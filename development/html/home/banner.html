About Us

Aquaculture without Borders (AWB) is founded under these basic principles: promoting sustainable agriculture, poverty eradication, eradication of malnutrition, increased aquacultural production, women and youth empowerment, collaboration and networking and technology transfer. ABW advocates for responsible sustainable aquaculture to feed our population and to protect the environment.
